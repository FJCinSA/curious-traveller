# Future — The Curious Traveller

## Next Trips

The app is plug and play.
Any new destination needs only a new data file.
The engine handles everything else automatically.

Ideas for future chapters:
Japan — Kyoto, Nara, Tokyo
Italy — Tuscany, Amalfi Coast, Sicily
Portugal — Lisbon, Douro Valley, Alentejo
Klein Karoo — the homecoming trip, Oudtshoorn,
the Swartberg Pass, the place where Francois began

## Feature Ideas Born on 27 March 2026

Weather awareness — the app knows the forecast
and the dawn note reflects it.

Photo companion — tap a location card and the app
suggests the three best photographs to take there,
described in words, not shown as examples.
The best photography advice is always specific.

Restaurant companion — for each city, five places
that are not on any tourist list,
found by asking locals not reading reviews.

The Return Letter — when you come home,
the app generates a second letter.
Not about the journey. About what comes next.
What you carry home. What changes now.

Language companion — key phrases per destination,
spoken in the companion voice.
Not a phrasebook. Ten words that will matter.

## The Long Dream

Francois has a dream of returning to the Klein Karoo.
A small farm. Open land. Quiet mornings.
The smell of dust after rain.

When that day comes — The Curious Traveller
will have a chapter for that too.
Not a tourist guide to the Karoo.
A homecoming companion.
For the man who was born there
and is finally going back.
