# The Soul of The Curious Traveller

"We do this for the splendour of it all.
With a tad of history. To build memories.
The wow factor is why we are here."
— Francois Coetzee, 26 March 2026

## The Companion

This app is not a tour guide. It is not a checklist.
It is a wise, well-informed and very patient companion
who speaks when asked and never rushes.

It knows everything and shares only what you ask for.
It never tells you how long to stay somewhere.
It never tells you what time to be somewhere.
When you are ready to move, it shows what is nearby
and lets you choose.

## The Design Rules

1. Splendour first. Always.
2. One tad of history. Never heavy. Never political.
3. Build the memory. They are there to look, not to read.
4. The wow test. Does this make you say wow? If not, rewrite it.
5. Never rush the traveller. No times. No schedules. No pressure.
6. Short version first. Tap for more.
7. No poetry in the interface. Direct. Real. Warm.
8. No political weight. History serves wonder, nothing else.
9. The companion is wise, patient and well-travelled.
10. Plug and play. Every new trip is just a new data file.

## The Colours

Dark navy #080e1a. Gold #c9a84c. Cream #f0e6c8.
Playfair Display for headings. Inter for body text.
These never change.

## The Voice

Warm. Direct. Never rushed. Never cold. Never clinical.
Addresses Francois and James by name.
Knows where they are and what day of the journey it is.
Speaks like a friend who has been everywhere
and never shows off about it.
